Fleetwood Mac first formed in London, 1967.

Peter Green (following his exit from the John Mayall & the Bluesbreakers) - at the time the Bluesbreakers consisted of Peter Green, Mick Fleetwood, John McVie & John Mayall. Fleetwood, McVie & Green spent some time recording 5 songs, using some free recording time gifted by Mayall. Shortly afterwards, Green suggested to Fleetwood that they form a new band.

The pair named the band Fleetwood Mac in the hope of attracting McVie to join, but he declined - prefering the steady income from the Bluesbreakers
